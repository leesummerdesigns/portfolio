FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data secure?

Absolutely. All your blood test data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. We never transmit, store, or have access to your personal health information on external servers (except for AI analysis, which is processed securely and not retained).

How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI analyzes your blood test parameters against standard medical ranges and provides insights based on established medical knowledge. However, this app is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

What types of blood tests can I upload?

Our app supports most standard blood test formats, including CBC (Complete Blood Count), CMP (Comprehensive Metabolic Panel), lipid panels, thyroid panels, vitamin levels, and more.

Do I need a subscription?

The app offers a free version with limited functionality. Premium features require a subscription, but you can try them free for 7 days with no credit card required.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel at any time through your App Store account settings. If you cancel during the free trial, you won't be charged.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains on your device even if you cancel your subscription. You can continue to view your stored tests, though some premium features will be limited.

What iOS version do I need?

BloodLog requires iOS 17.0 or later. Compatible with iPhone and iPad.
