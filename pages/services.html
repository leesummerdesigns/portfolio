Services

I work with companies and teams on everything from product design to bringing AI into the way they actually work. Sometimes that means rolling up my sleeves and designing alongside your team. Other times it's stepping back, looking at the bigger picture, and helping shape the strategy.

Here's where I'm most useful.

Product Design

End-to-end product design for digital products — from discovery and user research through to interaction design, prototyping, and delivery. I work closely with product and engineering teams to create experiences grounded in user behavior and business intent, without losing sight of quality or craft.

  • UX & Interaction Design
  • User Research & Testing
  • Prototyping & Handoff
  • Design Systems

Leading Design Teams

I help organisations build, grow, and run high-performing product design teams. Whether you need someone to step in as an interim design lead, structure a new team from scratch, or coach senior designers, I bring structure, clarity, and a culture of craft to design organisations at any stage.

  • Team Structure & Hiring
  • Design Leadership
  • Mentoring & Coaching
  • Design Culture

AI-Driven Working Process

I help product teams make the switch to an AI-augmented way of working — practically, not theoretically. From research synthesis and ideation to design system governance and documentation, I identify the right touchpoints and embed AI tools that actually stick, without disrupting what already works.

  • AI Tool Integration
  • Workflow Redesign
  • Team Training & Enablement
  • Process Documentation

AI Strategy for Business

Not every AI solution fits every business. I help companies evaluate AI approaches — from off-the-shelf tools to custom models — and decide what makes sense given their size, data maturity, and ambitions. The result is a grounded, realistic AI roadmap aligned with your actual business model and team capabilities.

  • AI Readiness Assessment
  • Tool & Platform Evaluation
  • AI Roadmapping
  • Stakeholder Alignment

Process Automation with AI

I help companies identify repetitive, manual processes that are ripe for automation and implement AI-powered solutions to handle them — from marketing and lead generation to reporting, content workflows, and internal operations. The goal is meaningful time savings and better scalability, not automation for its own sake.

  • Workflow Automation
  • Agentic AI Systems
  • Marketing & Lead Generation
  • Operational Efficiency
