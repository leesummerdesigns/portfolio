Hi I'm Louis,
a Product Expert

AI & User Experience

I'm a product designer with over 20 years of business experience, currently based in Zürich, Switzerland. I have a passion for creating seamless, impactful products grounded in a deep understanding of human behavior. Today, my work focuses on introducing AI-driven automations into digital organizations and training product designers to integrate AI technologies into their design process—from discovery to delivery. I help multidisciplinary teams use AI intelligently to improve efficiency, collaboration, and decision-making, empowering both companies and the people behind their products to achieve their goals more effectively.
